Lessons Available

Essential Driver Training

Essential Driver Training (EDT) is a structured training programme for learner drivers consisting of 12 driving sessions with an Approved Driving Instructor (ADI), or six sessions if you are eligible for reduced EDT.

Pre-Test Lessons

We offer pretest lessons which will prepare you better for the actual driving test Our pretests are designed to instill confidence and competency into our students, to ensure they have a better chance of passing their test at the first attempt. We identify areas of potential problem and provide correction advice when required. A pretest normally lasts 2 hrs and is conducted on an actual test route.

Hourly Lessons

Hourly lessons are available on request. They can be conducted on a test route, motorway or their local area. Contact us for more information
